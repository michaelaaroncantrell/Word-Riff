I'm a mathematician turned data scientist based in Chicago. After completing a PhD and a Post-Doc in pure mathematics, I turned my attention to solving real world problems with the help of computers and data. That is, data science.

Word Riff is my final project for my data science bootcamp, Metis. I had a great time putting this project together, and hope you enjoy playing with the tools as much as I do. I have to admit, I didn't expect to actually discover new music with the tools I built, but I am happy to be wrong. The musical mashups are really fun, too. For example, try mashing up NWA's "Straight Outta Compton" with Johnny Cash's "At Folsom Prison". Another good one is Bob Dylan's "Before the Flood" and Wu Tang Clan's "Enter The Wu-Tang (36 Chambers)".

Enjoy!
